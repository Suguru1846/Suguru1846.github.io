❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does Talk To Me Diary work?

Talk to the built-in AI just like chatting with a friend. Your daily conversations are saved as diary entries, and the app helps you reflect with built-in sentiment analysis.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations are only stored on your device and are not shared or used for training.

I lost my data. Can I recover it?

Currently, Talk To Me Diary does not support cloud backups. We recommend exporting or saving your entries manually for now.
